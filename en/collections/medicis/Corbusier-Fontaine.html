Title:

La Fontana della Barcaccia

High school:

Lycée Le Corbusier - Tourcoing (France)

Material:

Adhesif tape, digital printing, PMMA panels, steel, glue

Description

Located in the center of Rome, the Tridente's district holds emblematic locations, such as la Piazza di Spagna. This well-known district also holds la Fontana della Barcaccia, represented in this digitalized masterpiece.

The highlight was made on movement and fluidity, created by a work on different colors and thickness. This impression of movement is also strengthened by the layer superposition of this fountain's model.

Next to this fountain is an added project made with heated glass. This clever decoration's assembly was made with glass manufactured through different techniques (glass paste, glass frit, etc...) that gives life to the Esculape's Temple.
