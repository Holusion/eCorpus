Title:

Terra Materiali

High school:

Lycée Jacques Le Caron - Arras (France)

Materials:

Staff, stone, wood, glass, granit

Description

This Terra Materiali project brought together students and skills from diverse background, enabling them to strenghten both their artistic culture and their team spirit through collaboration.

Inspired by Arnaldo Pomodoro, this realization combines digital modeling and handmade knowledge. The whole structure is composed of a large variety of materials: wood, stone, glass, etc...
