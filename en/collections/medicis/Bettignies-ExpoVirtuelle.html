Title:

Virtual Exhibition

High school:

Lycée Louise de Bettignies - Cambrai (France)

Material:

Photographs

Description

Through this virtual exhibition, the viewer is given the opportunity to admire a series of photos inspired by Italian culture.

Firstly, on the left: two compositions inspired by vegetable-made portraits from the artist Arcimboldo. They were done using real fruits and vegetables displayed on a black background.

Finally, on the right: a series of three pictures presenting the same woman dressed as a chef, holding a pasta plate, each one in a different position. The first of the three poses is a tribute to the Mona Lisa by Da Vinci.
