Title:

The Music Box

High school:

Lycée d'Amyot d'Inville - Senlis (France)

Material:

Plywood, polystyrene, Acrylic and oil painting, Cardboard, Resin

Description

This model is mainly composed of plywood and cardboard, standing on a polystyrene base. The decorations are painted with acrylic paints, with the exception of the frescos, made with oil paints. The structure is inspired by those of the theaters from the Comedia dell'Arte, and multiple references concerning Italian culture can be found in this work's representations (Capitoline Wolf, Pierrot's sculpture, etc...).
