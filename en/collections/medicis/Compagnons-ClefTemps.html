Title:

The Key of Time

High school:

Les Compagnons du Devoir - Villeneuve d'Ascq (France)

Material:

wood (Ashwood, Padouk), Steel

Description

Combining both woodworking and steelworking mastery, the work was created like a collaborative masterpiece where the subject centers around narration of the environment. It includes various traditional techniques, historical references, and a contemporary plastic language.

By choosing Italian theather as the main subject, the high schoolers that worked on this project wanted to submit a hybrid realization: being both a functional structure and an illusionist device showing a reasoning centered around time.
