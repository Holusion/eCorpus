Description

Intended to display the consecrated host for the adoration of the faithful, this monstrance consists of a circular lunette surrounded by sunburst rays and surmounted by a cross. The base is adorned with vegetal motifs, as are the decorative elements encircling the monstrance. Red and white stones punctuate the overall decoration.
