Description

Intended to display the consecrated host for the adoration of the faithful, this monstrance consists of a circular lunette surrounded by sunburst rays and surmounted by a cross. The shaft is decorated with two winged angels and a tied sheaf of wheat. It rests on a quadrangular base with four lion-paw feet, connected by a frieze of palmettes.
