Name

Right stall.

Origins

Unknown.

Date

Beginning of 19th century.

Place of conservation

Church of Saint-Martin-Bellevue, Hautes-Savoie, France.

Dimensions

length 3,06 x height 1,07 x depth 0,70m.

Material

Waxed Walnut wood.

Description

This dark walnut choir stall, composed of five single seats, features hinged seats, flat armrests, and gently scrolled arm supports. Positioned along the wall of the choir, it was intended for monks or clerics participating in the liturgical offices.
