History

Donated by the Sisters of Notre-Dame of Namur.

Description

On the silver binding plates appear Christ in Majesty and the Crucifixion. The niello, embossing, and filigree are of remarkable quality. Within the interlace, one can discern a hunting scene, and to the left of Christ in Majesty, a representation of Hugo of Oignies himself, accompanied by the letters of his name. Also noteworthy is a chalcedony Gorgon's head set above the Crucifixion.
